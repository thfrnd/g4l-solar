Our Journey

Founded in 2003 in Makati City, JME Company was established under the visionary leadership of its founding President, Mr. Ramon Hernandez Jr. The company initially specialized in corporate giveaways and promotional items, quickly earning a reputation for quality, reliability, and innovation.

Over the years, JME expanded its footprint beyond Metro Manila, successfully penetrating key markets across the Visayas and Mindanao. Through consistent excellence and customer-centric service, the company not only met expectations—but consistently exceeded them—building long-term relationships with a growing base of satisfied clients.

As the 21st century ushered in the rapid rise of wireless and smart technologies, the company strategically evolved to stay ahead of industry trends. This transformation led to the birth of Gadgets 4 Lifestyle (G4L) on March 30, 2015.

G4L was established with a clear mission: to introduce cutting-edge, high-performance, and future-ready technologies to the market. Today, the company stands as a trusted and exclusive distributor of innovative solutions, including solar-powered security systems, portable generators, and advanced AI-integrated gadgets.

Driven by innovation and sustainability, G4L continues to empower LGU’s, Private Companies, and Cooperatives with reliable, eco-friendly, and technologically advanced products—positioning itself at the forefront of the modern lifestyle and energy revolution.
