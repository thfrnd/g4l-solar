Product Specifications

The V28 4G Dual Lens Security Camera combines 4G LTE connectivity, dual-lens monitoring, AI human detection, full-color night vision, and solar-powered operation into a reliable outdoor security solution for remote locations and everyday monitoring.

Key Features

4G LTE Connectivity

Stay connected anywhere without requiring Wi-Fi.

  • Stable 4G network connection
  • Ideal for remote locations
  • Supports Cat1 and Cat4 LTE networks
  • Remote monitoring via O-KAM Pro App

Dual Lens Monitoring

Monitor two different viewing areas simultaneously using:

  • Wide-angle panoramic camera
  • Tracking camera with zoom capability
  • Intelligent dual-lens linkage
  • Automatic zoom tracking

AI Human Detection

Advanced AI intelligently distinguishes human movement from other objects, minimizing false alarms.

  • Human detection
  • PIR motion detection
  • Custom detection zones
  • Intelligent alarm notifications

Full-Color Night Vision

Capture clear footage day and night with dual light source technology.

  • 11 infrared LEDs
  • 11 white LEDs
  • Automatic day/night switching
  • Full-color night vision

Solar Powered

Designed for long-term outdoor operation.

  • Includes solar panel
  • Built-in rechargeable battery
  • Energy-efficient low-power system
  • Continuous charging during daylight

Massive 8000mAh Battery

High-capacity rechargeable battery provides extended operation with intelligent power management.

  • Built-in 8000mAh battery
  • Low battery warning
  • Ultra-low standby power consumption

Two-Way Audio

Communicate remotely using the built-in microphone and speaker with real-time voice intercom through the mobile app.

Fast Event Recording

Starts recording within 400ms after motion detection to capture important events quickly.

Free Cloud Storage

Includes complimentary cloud storage for alarm recordings and also supports loop recording and TF card storage up to 256GB.

Smart Alarm System

Receive instant notifications when motion is detected.

  • Human detection alerts
  • PIR motion detection
  • Sound and light alarm
  • Custom alarm sounds
  • App push notifications

Remote Access

View live video and playback anytime using the O-KAM Pro mobile application with live viewing, playback, remote control, device management, and up to four simultaneous users.
