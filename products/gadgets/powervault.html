Product Details

Detailed specifications

The PowerVault Elite is a high-performance 25,000mAh portable power bank built for laptops, smartphones, tablets, and other USB-powered devices. Delivering up to 170W total output with 100W USB-C fast charging, it provides reliable power on the go while remaining compact, travel-friendly, and capable of charging multiple devices simultaneously.

Battery

  • Battery Capacity: 25,000mAh
  • Battery Energy: 90Wh
  • Battery Type: Lithium-Ion
  • Airline Approved: Yes (under 100Wh limit)

Charging Specifications

  • USB-C Input: Up to 100W PD Fast Charging
  • USB-C Output: Up to 100W PD Fast Charging
  • USB-A Output: Up to 33W Fast Charging
  • Maximum Combined Output: 170W
