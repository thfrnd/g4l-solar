Product Details

Product Specifications

Chronova X Smartwatch combines a large full-touch display, Bluetooth calling, smart notifications, health monitoring, and lifestyle tools into a versatile everyday wearable built for work, fitness, and travel.

Key Features

Large HD Full Touch Display

Enjoy a bright, vibrant display with intuitive full-touch controls for easy navigation, notifications, fitness tracking, and watch customization.

Bluetooth Calling

Stay connected without reaching for your phone.

  • Make and receive Bluetooth calls
  • Built-in microphone
  • Built-in speaker
  • View call history
  • Dial directly from the watch
  • Contact synchronization

Smart Notifications

Receive instant notifications from your smartphone, including incoming calls, SMS messages, WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, Twitter (X), email, and other supported app alerts.

Comprehensive Health Monitoring

Monitor your wellness throughout the day with built-in sensors including:

  • Real-time Heart Rate Monitoring
  • Blood Oxygen (SpO2) Monitoring
  • Blood Pressure Monitoring*
  • Sleep Monitoring
  • Stress Monitoring*
  • Sedentary Reminder
  • Women’s Health Tracking*

*Health metrics are intended for wellness and fitness purposes only and are not medical-grade measurements.

Multiple Sports Modes

Track your workouts with dedicated activity modes including walking, running, cycling, hiking, basketball, football, badminton, yoga, fitness training, and additional indoor and outdoor activities.

300mAh Long-Lasting Battery

Equipped with a 300mAh rechargeable battery providing up to 5-7 days of typical use, longer standby time depending on usage, and magnetic charging.

Customizable Watch Faces

Personalize your smartwatch with multiple built-in watch faces, app-based downloadable watch faces, and custom photo watch faces.

Bluetooth Music Control

Control your smartphone music directly from your wrist with play/pause, previous track, next track, and volume control.

Camera Remote Control

Use your smartwatch as a remote shutter for your smartphone camera, perfect for group photos, selfies, and tripods.

Smart Lifestyle Functions

Includes practical everyday tools such as weather forecast, alarm clock, stopwatch, timer, calculator, find phone, find watch, Do Not Disturb mode, raise-to-wake display, and breathing exercises.

IP68 Water Resistance

Designed to withstand sweat, rain, hand washing, and everyday splashes. Not suitable for swimming, diving, or hot showers.
