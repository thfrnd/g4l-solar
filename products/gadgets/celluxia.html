Detailed specifications

The CelluXia AI combines a powerful smartphone and a built-in projector in one innovative device. Featuring AI-powered capabilities, 1080P projection support, a high-transparency all-glass lens module, and laser projection technology, it delivers a large-screen viewing experience anytime, anywhere. Perfect for entertainment, presentations, gaming, and outdoor adventures, the CelluXia AI transforms any wall into your personal cinema.
