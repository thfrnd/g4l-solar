Product Details

Product Specifications

RaPower 1800 combines a 1024Wh LiFePO4 battery, 1800W pure sine wave inverter, smart charging, and broad output support into a portable power station built for dependable home backup, travel, and off-grid use.

Key Features

1800W Pure Sine Wave AC Output

Delivers 1800W continuous output with 3600W surge capacity, allowing it to power refrigerators, microwaves, power tools, coffee makers, televisions, laptops, CPAP machines, routers, drones, and many other household and outdoor devices.

High-Capacity 1024Wh LiFePO4 Battery

Built with premium LiFePO4 battery cells offering:

  • 1024Wh energy storage
  • Over 4,000 charge cycles
  • Approximately 80% capacity retention after 4,000 cycles
  • Superior thermal stability
  • Enhanced safety and long service life

Ultra-Fast AC Charging

Recharge from 0% to 100% in approximately 1.2 hours using AC power.

  • Maximum AC charging power: 1200W
  • High charging efficiency up to 96%

Solar Charging Ready

Ideal for off-grid applications using compatible solar panels.

  • Maximum solar charging input: 500W
  • MPPT solar charging
  • DC input voltage: 10-52V
  • Maximum PV input current: 11A

Vehicle Charging

Recharge while travelling via your vehicle.

  • Maximum car charging input: 120W

Seamless UPS Function

Provides an uninterruptible power supply (UPS) with an automatic switching time of approximately 10ms, ensuring connected devices continue operating during power outages.

SmartDrive Technology

Automatically adjusts inverter performance when compatible connected loads temporarily exceed the rated output, helping maintain reliable power delivery while protecting the system.

Pure Sine Wave Inverter

Produces clean, stable electricity with THD below 1.5%, making it suitable for sensitive electronics including computers, networking equipment, televisions, medical devices, and audio equipment.

Advanced Battery Management System (BMS)

Comprehensive battery protection continuously monitors system performance to maximize safety, battery life, and operational reliability.

Power Up to 11 Devices Simultaneously

A wide selection of AC, USB, and DC outputs allows multiple devices to be powered or charged at the same time.

Wireless Monitoring

Integrated Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity enables convenient monitoring and control through a compatible mobile application.

Built-in LED Emergency Light

Includes a 3W LED light for camping, emergency situations, and nighttime use.
