Product Details

Product Specifications

Solaris of Ra 5000 combines a 5120Wh LiFePO4 battery, 5000W pure sine wave inverter, smart app control, and heavy-duty output support into a high-capacity power solution built for demanding residential and commercial applications.

Key Features

5000W Pure Sine Wave Inverter

Provides 5000W continuous AC output with 9000W surge power, capable of powering high-load appliances including:

  • Refrigerators and freezers
  • Air conditioners
  • Induction cookers
  • Electric kettles
  • Microwaves
  • Washing machines
  • Power tools
  • Pumps
  • Computers and servers
  • Medical equipment

The pure sine wave output ensures safe operation for sensitive electronics.

Massive 5120Wh LiFePO4 Battery

Built with premium Lithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO4) battery technology offering:

  • 5120Wh battery capacity
  • Over 4,000 charge cycles
  • Approximately 80% capacity retention after 4,000 cycles
  • Superior thermal stability
  • Enhanced safety
  • Long operational lifespan

Expandable Battery System

Increase storage capacity from 5.12kWh standard up to 10.24kWh with an additional battery module, making it ideal for extended power outages and off-grid installations.

Ultra-Fast 3450W AC Charging

Recharge rapidly from an AC outlet with:

  • Maximum AC charging power: 3450W
  • High charging efficiency up to 93%
  • Significantly reduced recharge time compared to conventional power stations

1000W MPPT Solar Charging

Designed for efficient renewable energy charging.

  • Maximum solar input: 1000W
  • MPPT solar charging
  • DC input voltage: 12-80V
  • Maximum PV input current: 16A

Emergency Power Supply (EPS)

Provides 10ms seamless switching, automatically supplying backup power during grid outages to keep essential devices operating without interruption.

Smart App Control

Integrated Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity enables remote monitoring and control through the companion mobile application.

  • Battery monitoring
  • Power usage monitoring
  • Input and output management
  • System settings
  • Firmware updates

LCD Status Display

Large built-in LCD provides real-time information including:

  • Battery percentage
  • Input power
  • Output power
  • Remaining runtime
  • Charging status
  • System warnings

Pure Sine Wave Output

Clean electrical output with THD below 1.5%, stable voltage, and safe operation for sensitive electronics and medical equipment.

Multiple Charging Methods

Recharge via AC wall outlet, solar panels, or vehicle charging.

Built for Heavy-Duty Applications

Ideal for:

  • Home backup systems
  • Off-grid homes
  • RV and caravan power
  • Outdoor events
  • Construction sites
  • Emergency response
  • Mobile workshops
  • Commercial applications
