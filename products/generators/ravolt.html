Product Details

Product Specifications

RaVolt 800 combines a 512Wh LiFePO4 battery, 800W pure sine wave inverter, smart charging, and wide device compatibility into a compact portable power station built for home backup, travel, and outdoor use.

Key Features

800W Pure Sine Wave AC Output

Provides 800W continuous output with 1600W surge power, making it suitable for laptops, TVs, CPAP machines, mini-fridges, routers, power tools, drones, cameras, and other essential appliances.

Long-Life LiFePO4 Battery

Built with premium LiFePO4 battery cells offering:

  • 512Wh capacity
  • Over 4,000 charge cycles
  • 80% capacity retention after 4,000 cycles
  • Improved safety and thermal stability
  • Long service life

Ultra-Fast AC Charging

Recharge the battery quickly through a wall outlet with:

  • Maximum AC charging power: 550W
  • Approximately 1.2-hour full recharge
  • High charging efficiency up to 95%

Solar Charging Ready

Recharge using compatible solar panels for off-grid applications.

  • Solar input: Up to 300W
  • MPPT charging
  • DC input voltage: 12-60V
  • Maximum input current: 10A

Vehicle Charging

Recharge while travelling.

  • Car charging input: 120W
  • Compatible with 12V vehicle charging

SmartDrive Technology

Automatically manages overload conditions, allowing the inverter to safely power compatible devices beyond its rated output without sudden shutdowns.

Pure Sine Wave Inverter

Produces clean and stable AC power with THD below 1.5%, making it safe for sensitive electronics including computers, networking equipment, medical devices and audio/video equipment.

Advanced Battery Management System (BMS)

Intelligent protection system safeguards both the battery and connected devices through multiple protection mechanisms.

Multiple Output Ports

Power up to 10 devices simultaneously through:

  • AC outlets
  • USB-C PD ports
  • USB-A fast charging ports
  • 12V car socket
  • Dual DC5525 outputs

Portable Design

Compact, lightweight construction with integrated carry handle, making it ideal for:

  • Camping
  • Caravan/RV travel
  • Fishing
  • Outdoor events
  • Emergency preparedness
  • Mobile offices

Built-in LED Light

Integrated 3W LED light for emergency lighting and outdoor use.

Wireless Connectivity

Supports Wi-Fi and Bluetooth for app-based monitoring and control.
