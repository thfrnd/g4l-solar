Product Details

Product Specifications

AmunRa 3200 combines a 2048Wh LiFePO4 battery, 3200W pure sine wave inverter, smart charging, and wide device compatibility into a high-capacity portable power station built for demanding backup and off-grid use.

Key Features

3200W Pure Sine Wave AC Output

Delivers 3200W continuous power with an impressive 6400W surge capacity, making it suitable for powering refrigerators, microwaves, induction cookers, power tools, air conditioners, coffee machines, televisions, laptops, and other demanding appliances.

Massive 2048Wh LiFePO4 Battery

Built with premium lithium iron phosphate (LiFePO4) battery cells for exceptional longevity and safety.

  • 2048Wh battery capacity
  • 4,000+ charge cycles
  • Approximately 80% capacity retention after 4,000 cycles
  • Superior thermal stability
  • Enhanced safety and long service life

Ultra-Fast AC Charging

Recharge from empty to full in approximately 1.2-1.5 hours using high-speed AC charging.

  • Maximum AC charging power: 1800W
  • Charging efficiency up to 94%

High-Power MPPT Solar Charging

Designed for rapid off-grid charging with high-efficiency MPPT technology.

  • Maximum solar input: 1000W
  • MPPT efficiency over 99.9%
  • DC input voltage: 12-60V
  • Maximum PV input current: 20A

Vehicle Charging

Recharge while travelling using a standard vehicle outlet.

  • Maximum car charging input: 120W

EPS / UPS Backup Function

Features Emergency Power Supply (EPS) with an automatic switchover time of approximately 10ms, ensuring uninterrupted power for essential devices during outages.

SmartDrive Technology

Automatically adjusts inverter performance when connected loads temporarily exceed the rated output, allowing compatible appliances to continue operating safely and efficiently without unnecessary shutdowns.

Advanced Battery Management System (BMS)

A dual-core intelligent BMS continuously monitors battery performance and provides comprehensive protection for maximum safety and reliability.

Power Up to 16 Devices Simultaneously

Equipped with 16 output ports across AC, USB-C, USB-A, and DC connections, allowing multiple devices to be powered or charged at the same time.

Smart App Control

Built-in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity enables remote monitoring and control through the compatible mobile application.

Built-in LED Emergency Light

Integrated 3W LED light provides convenient illumination for camping, emergencies, and outdoor use.
